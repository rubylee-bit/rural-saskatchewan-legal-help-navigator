Rural Saskatchewan Help Navigator

A simple place to begin when you are unsure where to look for legal, health, financial, housing, employment, newcomer, or community support.

Many people do not know whether their problem is legal, health-related, financial, or social-service related. In rural areas in particular, it can be difficult to find the right pertaining information or resources, but there is nothing wrong with not knowing where to start.

Find a starting point for legal and community support in Yorkton and rural Saskatchewan.

This is a student-led public legal information page that seeks to help residents across Saskatchewan, especially rural communities, locate the kind of legal help that they may need, whether that be Legal Aid, lawyer directories, mental health and addictions supports, crisis resources, housing, employment, newcomer, and community services.

This website does not give legal advice. It is a starting point to help residents find resources they may need and is not a substitute for professional legal, medical, financial, or social support.
