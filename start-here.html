Start Here

Answer a few simple questions to find a safer and clearer place to begin.

Many legal problems may involve several aspects, such as housing, employment, mental health, addictions, family, immigration, or finances. This page is designed to help you find a starting point and guide you in the right direction.

Step 1 of 5

Is anyone in immediate danger?

Immediate danger

Call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department.

Step 2 of 5

Is this an emotional crisis or suicide-related concern?

Emotional crisis or suicide-related concern

Call or text 988, or call 811.

Yes, go to Legal Help
