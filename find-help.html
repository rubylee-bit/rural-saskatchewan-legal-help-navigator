Select the option that best matches what is happening. Many problems overlap, so more than one section may be helpful.

This page is organized around real-life situations rather than legal categories. You do not need to know whether your problem is legal, financial, health-related, or social-service related before looking for a starting point.

My situation does not fit into one category.

Many problems affect more than one part of life. That is normal, and it does not mean you are looking in the wrong place.

  • Losing a job may affect housing, finances, and mental health.
  • Family violence may involve legal, housing, safety, and counselling supports.
  • Immigration concerns may also involve employment, housing, or language services.
