Legal Help

Find starting points for legal advice, legal information, lawyer directories, court resources, and low-cost legal support in Saskatchewan.

This page does not provide legal advice. It provides public legal information and links to organizations that may help you find legal support. If you need advice about your specific situation, contact Legal Aid Saskatchewan, a licensed lawyer, or another qualified legal professional.

Legal advice and low-cost legal help

Legal Advice

Legal Aid Saskatchewan — Yorkton

Legal Aid Saskatchewan may provide free or low-cost criminal and family legal services to financially eligible residents who cannot afford a private lawyer.

  • Address: 120 Smith St E, Yorkton, SK S3N 3V3
  • Phone: (306) 786-1440
  • May assist with: criminal, family, child welfare, domestic abuse, and other eligible matters
Visit Legal Aid Saskatchewan
Free Legal Clinic

Pro Bono Law Saskatchewan

If you earn too much for Legal Aid but still cannot afford private lawyer fees, Pro Bono Law Saskatchewan may be another option to explore.

  • Free legal clinics with volunteer lawyers
  • Up to one hour of free legal advice
  • Phone appointments may be available for rural residents
  • Call: 1-855-833-7257
  • Typical wait time: 3–6 weeks
Visit Pro Bono Law Saskatchewan

Legal Information

Public Legal Education Association of Saskatchewan

PLEA provides plain-language public legal information about legal rights, obligations, and court procedures.

  • Useful if you need general legal information
  • Not a substitute for legal advice
  • May help you understand common legal topics before contacting a lawyer

Court and government legal resources

Courts

Courts of Saskatchewan

Saskatchewan’s court system includes the Provincial Court, the Court of King’s Bench, and the Court of Appeal. The official court website provides information about court locations, processes, and court-related resources.

  • Provincial Court: criminal, youth, traffic, municipal bylaw, and small claims matters
  • Court of King’s Bench: serious criminal, civil, and family law trials
  • Court of Appeal: reviews decisions from lower courts
Visit Courts of Saskatchewan
Yorkton Court

Provincial Court of Saskatchewan — Yorkton

The Provincial Court handles many criminal, youth, traffic, municipal bylaw, and small claims matters.

  • Address: 120 2nd Avenue North, Yorkton
  • Phone: (306) 786-1400

Family law and safety-related legal information

Family Law

Family Law Information Centre

Family law in Saskatchewan may involve divorce, separation, child or spousal support, parenting, property division, and related issues.

  • Free family law resources, forms, and legal coaching may be available
  • Call: 1-888-218-2822
  • Early Family Dispute Resolution may be required before going to court
Visit Family Law Saskatchewan
Child Support

Saskatchewan Child Support Service

The Saskatchewan Child Support Service provides an administrative, out-of-court process to calculate or adjust child support payments.

  • Phone: (306) 787-5042
  • Toll-free: 1-833-825-1445
  • Email: childsupportservice@gov.sk.ca
View Child Support Information

Questions to consider before contacting a lawyer

  • What kind of legal issue do I need help with?
  • Do I need legal advice, or am I only looking for general legal information?
  • Can I afford a private lawyer, or should I look into Legal Aid Saskatchewan or other alternatives?
  • Have I checked that the lawyer or legal professional is licensed through an official directory?
  • Do I need help in a specific language?
  • Do they offer phone or virtual consultations?
  • Is there a consultation fee?
  • What documents should I prepare before speaking with them?
